Peck your friends when they take pictures.™

What is Peck?
Peck is a mobile app the helps you share experiences in real-time with friends using your photos.

Why do I want to use Peck?
We provide the best way to connect with your friends over shared experiences by the location where you took photos in a private and fun experience.

How do I use Peck?
We've made a simple overview of our features and how to use them that you can view in our How To Use Peck guide.

Who built Peck?
Peck was built by the team at Threefold Photos, Inc. and the creators of photo sharing pioneer Webshots.

How do I use Peck?
Peck is currently in beta and available on an invite basis. If you have received an invite, you can follow these steps to start using it:

  1. Install our app through the link provided.
  2. After you install our app, we map your photos, connect you to friends and enable you to share the location where you have and are taking photos.
  3. When you open our app, you’ll see where you and your friends have photos on a map.
  4. When you take a photo, we’ll notify your friends that you are taking photos and show approximately where on a map.
