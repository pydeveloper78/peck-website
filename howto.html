The Map shows you where to find your friends and photos.

The Map is your home view. It's the place where you can see where you took all those pictures on your phone or see where your friends are taking pictures. Just click on the dots or birds on the map to see what's going on.

Hold down the Map icon to Reload, Zoom Out, Zoom to Location or Move to Location. Or simply scroll through map using your fingers like other map applications.

Peck your Friends to see pictures and chat with friends.

Here's what's different about Peck. We let you know when your friends are doing something active. In our first version, that's taking a picture.

To see what is going on with your friends, just tap their name and "peck" your friend's bird to chat or see their pictures. Click Edit in the top right corner for more friend settings.

Flocks are dynamic group chats that go away after a few hours.

To start a flock just peck a friend from the map or your Friends list. After you peck you will be included in a dynamic group chat with others who have pecked into the conversation.

After a few hours, the conversation disappears while new flocks are formed over other events as they happen.

The You tab is where you can change your bird and other settings.

On your You tab you can update all of your settings for Peck. Including picking out a new bird for your profile! As we add more features your profile settings will become more important.
